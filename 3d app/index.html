RED VOID

3D Survival Shooter
PLAY GAME

About

Experience a true first-person shooter with PUBG-style free look controls! Navigate a neon grid environment in first-person view and survive against waves of glowing enemies. Use your shooting skills, quick reflexes, and 360° situational awareness to rack up kills!

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Move in any direction
  • Mouse: Look around 360° (first-person free look - always active)
  • Left Click: Shoot fireballs at enemies
  • ESC: Exit pointer lock

Features

  • First-person shooter with 360° free look (always-on)
  • Enhanced 3D graphics with dynamic lighting and shadows
  • Glowing enemies for better visibility
  • Object pooling for smooth performance
  • Adaptive spawn system
  • Configurable settings (shadows, particles, spawn rate)
  • Neon aesthetic with atmospheric fog
