RED VOID

3D Survival Shooter
PLAY GAME

About

Navigate a neon grid environment and survive against waves of enemies. Use your shooting skills and quick reflexes to rack up kills!

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Move
  • Mouse: Aim (pointer lock)
  • Left Click: Shoot
  • Right Click: Hold for free look
  • F: Toggle free look
  • ESC: Exit pointer lock

Features

  • Object pooling for smooth performance
  • Adaptive spawn system
  • Configurable settings (shadows, particles, spawn rate)
  • Neon aesthetic with atmospheric fog
